Build Your Closet

Add everything you own.
Let AI do the typing.

Four ways to build your closet — none of them require manually filling out a form.

📸

Smart Auto-Tagging

Take a photo of any garment and the AI identifies the category, colour, fabric, brand, and style tags — instantly, in the background. A "Tagging…" badge shows in your grid; you can keep shopping your closet while it works. If tagging ever fails, a yellow Retry badge appears on the item.

AI Vision
🔗

Import from Product URL

Paste any product link — ASOS, Zara, H&M, Net-a-Porter, anywhere. The app fetches the page directly from your device, distils the product details, and the AI pre-fills every field. Review the result, tap Save. Done.

No proxy needed
🧾

Receipt Scanner

Upload a screenshot of any order confirmation email or app receipt. The AI reads every clothing line item, filters out shipping, taxes, gift cards, and gift wrapping, then shows you a review list. Select what you want, tap Add — all items land in your closet at once.

Bulk import
🤳

Background Removal

When you add a photo, the AI runs on-device background removal to isolate the garment against a clean transparent background. Your closet grid stays consistent and clutter-free — no distracting bedspread or floor visible behind your shirt.

On-device · Private
AI Styling

A stylist that knows
your wardrobe cold.

Every AI feature is powered by your actual closet and style profile — not generic internet advice.

✨

Outfit Scoring

Take or upload a photo of any outfit. The AI scores it 0–10, explains what's working (colour harmony, proportion, occasion fit), and tells you exactly what to change. Scoring tone is adjustable in settings: Gentle, Balanced, or Honest — from encouraging coach to fashion editor.

Rate any outfit
💬

AI Concierge

A natural-language bar on the home screen. Ask anything: "What should I wear to a rooftop tonight?", "Pack for a long weekend in New York", "Does this blazer work with wide-leg trousers?" The AI routes your question to the right feature and pre-fills it with context from your closet and calendar.

Home screen access
🔍

Scan an Item

Point your camera at any item — yours, a friend's, or one in a shop. The AI identifies it, checks your closet for what you already own that pairs well, and returns outfit suggestions. It also surfaces similar items you could add and a shopping section for pieces that would complete the look.

Match + pair
📷

Inspo Snap

Screenshot an outfit from Instagram, a magazine, or anywhere online. Upload it and the AI decomposes it into individual pieces, matches each to items you already own, and shows you how close you can get with your current wardrobe. Unmatched pieces link to shop suggestions.

Recreate any look
Plan Ahead

Always know what you're
wearing before you open your closet.

The AI reads your calendar, knows your upcoming trips, and has outfit ideas ready before you ask.

🧳

Trip Planner

Enter a destination and the occasions you have scheduled — dinner, beach, hiking, a wedding, the flight home. The AI builds a complete outfit plan using pieces you already own, one outfit per event. You can refine it with natural-language follow-ups: "Make day 2 more casual", "I forgot I have a black-tie dinner."

Occasion-aware packing
📆

Calendar Integration

Grant read-only access to your calendar and the planner automatically pulls your next 7 days of events into outfit planning. A work presentation, a date night, a friend's birthday brunch — the AI knows what's coming and factors it in. It never creates or edits your calendar events.

Read-only · Private
📋

Weekly Outfit Planner

The Planner tab generates outfit ideas for the whole week based on your calendar, the weather forecast for your area, and your style profile. Tap "Plan my week" for a full structured plan, or "Quick outfit ideas" for a lighter set of suggestions when you just want inspiration.

Whole-week view
🎭

Dress for the Occasion

Tell the AI what you're doing — "dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant", "a casual Friday in the office", "a summer wedding in Tuscany" — and it builds a specific outfit recommendation from your wardrobe, with scoring, alternatives, and a shop section for any gaps.

Any occasion
Know Your Style

The more you use it,
the better it gets.

Your style profile, worn history, and sizing all feed the AI — making every suggestion sharper over time.

🎨

Style Profile & Quiz

A 5-step onboarding quiz that builds your taste signal: the vibes that resonate with you, style icons whose wardrobes you'd raid, places and occasions you typically dress for, and a comfort-to-statement slider. Every AI prompt is personalised to this profile. Retake the quiz anytime in Settings.

Personalised AI
📅

Style Memory (Worn Log)

After each outfit, log it with a photo, the items you wore, a note, and a date. Over time the AI learns your real habits — what you actually reach for, what sits unworn, which combinations you keep returning to. The last 20 entries feed every AI feature automatically.

AI learns your habits
📏

My Sizes

Multi-select sizing per category: Tops, Bottoms, Full body, and Shoes. Each category has its own measurement-system dropdown (US, EU, UK, IT) and lets you select multiple sizes — because sizing is inconsistent across brands. The AI uses this for fit suggestions only, never to gate or filter recommendations.

Better fit suggestions
🏷️

Smart Search

Every tag the AI applies is searchable. Type "green linen" and see only green linen items. Type "formal navy" and get every navy item tagged formal. Search is token-AND, so combining terms narrows results precisely. The search index updates instantly when you edit tags.

Token-AND search

Automatic Cloud Backup

Every time you add, edit, or delete an item, the change syncs to the cloud in the background. Your closet photos, metadata, worn log, saved looks, and style profile are all included. If you lose your phone tomorrow, reinstall and sign in — everything comes back.

Includes photos
📱

Multi-Device Sync

Sign in on any iPhone and your full closet is there. Changes made on one device appear on all others automatically. Offline-first: edits made without a connection sync as soon as you reconnect — nothing is ever lost.

Offline-first sync
📲

Home Screen Widget

A small or medium widget on your home screen shows your closet count and the last outfit you logged. Tap it to open the app, or use the quick-log deep link to jump straight to the Log Outfit sheet — without unlocking the app first.

Free for all users
