### TT-01 · "Nothing to wear" (Relatable / POV)
**Target:** 18-35 fashion-curious, closet-overwhelmed  
**Format:** 20-25s, talking to camera + phone demo  
**Tone:** Funny, relatable, satisfying reveal

```
[0-3s — HOOK, face fill frame, slightly exasperated]
"I have 200 pieces of clothing and I still stand here every
morning like I have NOTHING to wear."

[3-8s — cut to full closet shot / scrolling through racks]
"Every. Single. Day."

[8-16s — cut to phone screen, demo the app]
"Then I downloaded this app. I took a photo of what I was
gonna wear, asked the AI to rate it—"

[16-20s — show score: 9.2/10, AI feedback on screen]
"—and it gave me a 9.2 out of 10 with specific reasons why.
Like a REAL stylist."

[20-24s — back to face, delighted]
"It's called MyCloset: Ai. It's free. Link in bio."

[24-25s — hold on app on phone]
```

**Caption:**
```
finally have a reason to get dressed in the morning 👏 #myclosetai #outfitoftheday #styletips #wardrobeorganization #AIstyle #gettingdressed #ootd
```

## Audience Targeting (Paid Ads)

### Meta (Instagram/Facebook)

**Core audiences to test:**
- Women 18-35, interests: Fashion, Clothing, Shopping, Wardrobe organization
- Lookalike: App Store fashion app downloaders
- Behavioral: Frequent online shoppers (ASOS, Zara, H&M purchasers)
- Interest stack: Style + Organization (both — this is the sweet spot)

**Creative angle by audience:**
| Audience | Best hook |
|----------|-----------|
| Fashion / OOTD | "Rate my outfit" angle (TT-02) |
| Organization | "I catalogued my whole closet" (TT-04) |
| Travelers | "AI packed my vacation" (TT-03) |
| Online shoppers | "Receipt import hack" (TT-05) |

### TikTok Ads

**Spark Ads** (boost organic posts): Start with your top-performing organic videos, then put $20-50/day behind each one to let TikTok find the audience for you. This is cheaper than TopView/In-Feed before you have data.

**In-Feed format:** All scripts above work as-is. Lead with the hook on the first frame (text overlay + voice), no silent intros.
